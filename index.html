The Story

About Caroline

I began my journey in the circus arts with prop manipulation back in 2014, and now have over a decade of experience in flow arts. In 2018 I started my aerial journey and soon moved to Las Vegas to take my circus career to the next level. After training with world-renowned instructors, I became a professional circus artist in 2020.

Since then I've performed all over the country — at some of the largest events in Nevada including EDC, Formula 1, NASCAR, Burning Man, Las Vegas PRIDE, the Demolition of the Historic Tropicana, and the Grand Reopening of the Palms — sharing stages with artists like Marshmello, Tiësto, RÜFÜS DU SOL, and Chris Lake.

My signature act is the Fire Aerial Pentagram, but the collection runs deep: Dragon Hoop, LED Clubs, Rhinestone Quad Hoops, LED Lyra in LED heels, stiltwalking with flow props or fire breathing, Fire Lyra with lycopodium — and the new Digital Fire concept, born in 2026. I design and sew my own costumes, and my entertainment company can provide additional performers and equipment for any booking.

I'm a sponsored performer for the fire performance companies Juggling Calling and Pyrotechnique Las Vegas, and I run the Vegas Fire Dancers community on Instagram.
